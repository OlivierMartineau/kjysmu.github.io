Jaeyong Kang
Post-doctoral Researcher, KNUT

  • Google Scholar Page

I am a research scientist at Google working with Cordelia Schmid on research problems involving both vision and language. I completed Ph.D. in 2020 under the supervision of Bohyung Han and Minsu Cho at POSTECH. Before that I was advised by Gary Geunbae Lee at the same school for M.S. During my Ph.D., I interned at Disney Research, Google and Facebook working with Leonid Sigal, Jack Sim, Radu Soricut and Peter Vajda. My research interests primarily lies in the areas of computer vision and natural language processing, especially in the intersections of these areas.
